About MySemester

MySemester is a tool that helps university students stay on top of their academics. You can organize courses, keep track of grades, and see how your GPA is shaping up.

Features

  • Smart GPA tracking across multiple grading systems
  • Streamlined course management
  • Personalization options for themes and layout
  • Notifications (coming soon)
  • Local data storage for complete privacy

Built With

MySemester is developed with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, using Ionicons for interface elements.

Future Plans

  • Device synchronization
  • Advanced GPA analytics
  • AI-powered grade prediction
  • Secure cloud backup
  • Full mobile optimization
